About Me

A Coloradan by birth, I got my B.S. in Earth Sciences at Duke University in North Carolina, and my M.S. in Geochemistry at the University of Wisconsin, Madison. I have recently relocated to the Bay Area where I am enjoying the moderate temperatures and great coffee.

I like to do all the usual outdoorsy things: rock climbing, hiking, bug-watching and snake-catching. My favorite sitting-down activities are reading and writing. After spending a year as the marketing coordinator for a pioneer museum, I've gotten pretty into historical sewing, too.

My "quirk" is that I love Pokemon! My favorite is #181, Ampharos.
